Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add cream and mix well. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Crush kasuri methi between your palms and add to the gravy.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
