White Sauce Pasta with Loaded Veggies

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 1 cup mixed veggies (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, corn)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, sauté the mixed veggies in a little butter until tender. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and add minced garlic. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add flour and cook for 2 minutes, stirring continuously.
  5. Gradually add milk, whisking to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add grated cheese, salt, pepper, oregano, and chili flakes. Mix well.
  7. Add the cooked pasta and veggies to the sauce. Toss to coat evenly.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with extra cheese and herbs.
