About Me

Hi, I'm Sai Shraddha

I'm a student of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at SIES Nerul College. While I'm passionate about technology and data, my heart truly lies in the kitchen. Cooking has always been my escape, my creative outlet, and my way of bringing people together.

I'm originally from Chennai, a city known for its rich culinary heritage, and I recently moved to Mumbai. This shift has inspired me to explore and share the diverse cuisines of India. From the spicy flavors of the South to the rich gravies of the North, I'm on a mission to bring the taste of India to your kitchen.

AkshayaPatra is my way of sharing my love for cooking with the world. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner, I hope my recipes inspire you to try something new and fall in love with cooking, just like I did.

My Mission

My goal is to create a platform where food lovers can discover easy-to-follow recipes, cooking tips, and inspiration. I want to showcase the incredible diversity of Indian cuisine and help you recreate authentic flavors in your own kitchen. Whether it's a traditional family recipe or a modern twist on a classic, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

Join Me on This Culinary Journey!

Explore my collection of recipes and start cooking today. Let's bring the flavors of India to your table!
