Kozhakattai

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup grated jaggery
  • 1/4 cup coconut (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 tsp ghee
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Heat water in a pan and add a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil.
  2. Gradually add rice flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Cook until the mixture thickens and forms a dough-like consistency.
  4. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly. Knead into a smooth dough.
  5. In a separate pan, melt jaggery with a little water. Strain to remove impurities.
  6. Add grated coconut and cardamom powder to the jaggery syrup. Cook until thick.
  7. Take small portions of the dough, flatten them, and place a spoonful of the jaggery mixture in the center.
  8. Shape into dumplings and steam for 10-12 minutes.
  9. Serve warm, brushed with a little ghee.
